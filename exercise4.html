HTML Exercise 2 Solution 4 - Striking through a sentence


Exercise

Implement the strikethrough effect on a sentence.


Solution

To strike through a word or a sentence in HTML you use the strike through element ( <s> </s> )

There is also another element which shows the strike thorough but means the text has been deleted. It is done with the delete tag ( <del> </del> )

        <!DOCTYPE html>
        <html lang="en">
            <head>
                <meta charset="UTF-8">
                <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
                <title>HTML Exercise 2 Solution 4 </title>
            </head>
            <body>
                <p><s>I am a Strike through sentence</s> </p>
                <p><del>I am a deleted sentence</del> </p>
            </body>
        </html>

Output

I am a Strike through sentence

I am a deleted sentence
